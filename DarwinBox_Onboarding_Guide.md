## 📊 SLIDE 6: ASSIGN BUDDY & SPOC
```
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
         STEP 4: ASSIGN BUDDY & SPOC
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

TWO IMPORTANT ASSIGNMENTS:

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

👥 ASSIGN A BUDDY

What is a Buddy?
A buddy is a colleague who helps guide the new employee
during their first days/weeks.

How to Assign:
1️⃣  Click on the "Buddies" field
2️⃣  Search and select a colleague
3️⃣  The buddy will support them during onboarding

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

🎯 ASSIGN AN ONBOARDING SPOC

What is SPOC?
SPOC = Single Point of Contact
The main person responsible for the onboarding process.

How to Assign:
1️⃣  Click on the "Onboarding SPOC" field
2️⃣  Select the responsible person
3️⃣  They will oversee the entire onboarding process
```

## 🎯 KEY TIPS FOR NEW EMPLOYEES

**📌 IMPORTANT REMINDERS:**

✓ **Save your login credentials** - You'll need them for future logins

✓ **Complete tasks on time** - Your buddy and SPOC will guide you

✓ **Check notifications regularly** - Stay updated on your onboarding progress

✓ **Explore the dashboard** - Get familiar with available apps and resources

✓ **Be organized** - Keep track of important dates and deadlines
